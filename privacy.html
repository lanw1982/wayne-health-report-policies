Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 June 2026

Health Report is a private local application used by the owner to access their own Oura health data for personal morning reports.

Data Collected

The application may access Oura data authorized by the owner, including sleep, readiness, activity, heart rate, workout, and SpO2 data.

Use of Data

The data is used only to generate personal health and training summary reports for the owner.

Data Sharing

The application does not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Data may be stored locally on the owner's personal computer for personal analysis and report generation.

Data Deletion

The owner may delete any locally stored data at any time from their personal computer.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the application owner at the contact email listed in the Oura developer application.
